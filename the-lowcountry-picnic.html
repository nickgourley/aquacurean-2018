SAVANNAH, GEORGIA

August 9-12, 2018

The Lowcountry Picnic, "Life on the Water": Friday, August 10, 2018

Location: Westin Savannah Harbor, Boardwalk & Harbor Lawn

Time: 6:30 pm to 9:30 pm

Tickets: $60 per person. $45 per person advance, limited availability through May 31st.

Event Type: Outdoors; rain or shine. Food & beverages samples included and for purchase.

Attire & Information: Summer casual. Restricted to 21 years and over.

Overview: We provide the atmosphere, you provide the appetite. Join us for this river-side evening of live entertainment and great food experiences as we toast the end of summer. Presented on the Westin Savannah Harbor boardwalk and luxurious lawns, relax along the Savannah River and the setting sun while enjoying seafood and delicious accompaniments. Resist the temptation to slow-dance the night away as our live southern music provides an engaging back drop to this ultimate sea-foodie experience.

 

Patrons will receive a bucket of steamed oysters along with tasting samples from our Big Green Egg celebrity chef stations along with a variety of samples from our sponsoring beverage vendors. Providing the perfect complement to this engaging evening, a host of local craft beer and cocktails will be available for purchase. This will be one event you won't want to miss. How great is that?
