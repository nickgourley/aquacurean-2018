SAVANNAH, GEORGIA

August 9-12, 2018

Coastal Chefs Table: Thursday, August 9, 2018

Location: Grand Ballroom, Westin Savannah Harbor

Time: 5:30 pm to 8:30 pm

Tickets: $125 per person. $90 per person advance, limited availability through May 31st.

Event Type: Indoors; all-inclusive food & beverage

Attire & Information: Smart casual and/or dress. Restricted to 21 years and over.

Overview: Our kick-off event, this premier dinner features a seated 5-course culinary journey with paired wines, cocktails and spirits. Our talented team of Westin chefs will each partner with a guest chefs from lowcountry high-end eateries . Together they will create each course to pair perfectly with the selection of wines and spirits fashioned in partnership with our certified wine and spirit experts. Guests will delight in the masterful re-imagined Westin ballroom as they dine and are entertained by these world renowned chefs and mixologists.

 

An evening unlike any other, this event is an annual sell-out...and for good reason. With chef interactions, live audio and video showcasing the kitchen preparations, guests will enjoy the complete culinary experience of this stunning presentation.
